Ezra Hotel & Community Center

A New Landmark at the Gateway to Heliopolis

TypeHotel / Community
LocationHeliopolis, Cairo
Year2023
ToolsRevit · AutoCAD · 3ds Max · Photoshop
StatusAcademic Project

Overview

Ezra is a 200+ guest hotel and community center situated adjacent to Cairo International Airport, at the threshold between the historic suburb of Heliopolis and the city's expanding airport district. The project was conceived as a civic anchor, a building that serves the traveller in transit while also belonging fully to the neighbourhood it inhabits.

The brief called for an integration of hospitality and community programming, a condition that shaped every aspect of the design, from the organisation of the ground floor to the distribution of shared amenities across the building's vertical section.

Organisation

The ground floor is entirely public, an open arcade leads to a community hall, co-working space, and market stalls accessible to Heliopolis residents regardless of whether they are guests of the hotel. Above, a planted podium separates the public base from the private hotel tower, creating a rooftop amenity level shared between both programmes.

The facade draws from Heliopolis's early twentieth-century architectural heritage, arched openings, projecting balconies, and a masonry base, reinterpreted through a contemporary lens that reads as familiar without being imitative.
